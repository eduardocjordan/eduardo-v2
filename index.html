Eduardo Castro is a Brand Strategist with thirteen years building consumer brands across Mexico and Latin America. He has led strategy for PepsiCo, Johnson & Johnson, and Grupo Mariposa — shaping how millions of consumers experience some of the region's most iconic products.

He treats brand strategy as translation work: between business goals and human desire, between quantitative truth and cultural intuition, between global frameworks and the deeply local.
